About Us!

KoshyTea is a community gathering place. First established in 2021, KoshyTea was founded by humans and is a cultural hub for artists, activists, writers, thinkers and dreamers. We have updated our safety standards to exceed COVID regulations. Unfortunately, we do not allow indoor dining, however you can use our curbside pickup service and place your order using our KoshyTea mobile app!
